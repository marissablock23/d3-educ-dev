Is Education a Binding Constraint to Economic Growth?

The purpose of this tool is to analyze human capital and determine if it is a binding constraint to economic growth, using the growth diagnostics methodology.

What is a binding constraint?

At a basic level, a binding constraint is something that is supply constrainted relative to demand. A full analysis requires an investigation into all branches of the growth diagnostic tree.
